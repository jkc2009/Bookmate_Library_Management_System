Library Rules

  • Membership is required to borrow books.
  • Books can be borrowed for a period of 14 days.
  • A late fee will be charged for overdue books.
  • Please handle the books with care. Any damage must be reported.
  • Silence must be maintained within the library premises.
  • Eating and drinking are not allowed in the library.
  • Personal belongings should not be left unattended.
  • The library is not responsible for any loss of personal items.
